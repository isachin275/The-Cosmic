Child Safety Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

At The Cosmic, we are fully committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Zero-Tolerance Policy

We have a strict zero-tolerance policy toward CSAE. Any content, behavior, or user that promotes or depicts CSAE will be immediately removed and reported to the appropriate authorities.

Detection and Moderation

  • We utilize automated detection tools and human moderation to identify potential violations.
  • Flagged content is reviewed promptly and, if found in violation, permanently removed.
  • We work with legal authorities and child protection organizations such as NCMEC when required.

Reporting Abuse

Users can report harmful or abusive content or behavior directly in the app or by emailing us at support@thecosmic.online.

Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding child safety and content moderation, including data sharing with law enforcement when necessary.

Ongoing Commitment

We regularly review and update our policies to improve child safety protections and keep our community secure.

For questions or concerns, please contact us at support@thecosmic.online.
